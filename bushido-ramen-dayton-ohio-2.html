Review

The broth had a tonkotsu flavor but was a bit thin and not served piping hot. The noodles had a chewy texture. There were too many toppings, and I felt that corn didn’t really go well with it. On the positive side, the seasoned egg was flavorful and delicious, and the tofu I ordered instead of Pork Chashu was also well-seasoned and tasty.

Review

It was good chicken shoyu ramen. The block of butter placed on top was quite memorable. The soup was a chicken-based shoyu broth, but the soy sauce flavor wasn’t too strong. As expected, the soup wasn’t piping hot. The chashu had a nice, savory flavor and was really tasty.
This ramen usually costs $14.89, but this month it’s on sale for $13 including a drink, which makes the price and taste pretty good overall. I’d like to come back again to try their other ramen options.

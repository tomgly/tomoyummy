Review

The ramen looked cheap at first, but the taste was actually decent.
It had a solid tonkotsu flavor with a hint of saltiness. Unfortunately, it seemed like it was defrosted from frozen, so the overall temperature was lukewarm and some noodles were stuck together.
At $11, it’s a casual and convenient option if you get a bit hungry after playing at Round1.

Review

I saw this matcha ramen last week and was curious, so I decided to try it this time.
It’s definitely one of the most unique bowls of ramen I’ve had, but it turned out to be more delicious than I expected.
The broth had a matcha flavor, but it wasn’t overpowering, just the good balance.
The chashu and egg were the same as before, both good seasoned and tasty.
If you have the chance, I definitely recommend trying it this month while it’s still on sale!.
